This work explores transformer-based models combined with attention mechanisms to improve diagnostic accuracy in chest X-ray classification, achieving 5-8% performance gains over traditional CNNs on benchmark datasets [web:31].

- Uncertainty quantification for clinical decision support
- Real-time inference optimized for edge devices
